Savings Flow

Meet Chrumek

A savings assistant, not a spending tracker.

1. You log a saving: skip a purchase, pick a cheaper option, or cancel a subscription.
2. Chrumek reminds you in the evening: transfer that amount to savings or drop it into your piggy bank.
3. You confirm the transfer in the app: only then does it count toward progress.
4. History and charts grow: you see results and unlock new Chrumek levels.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to connect my bank?

No. A separate savings account or piggy bank is enough. You confirm transfers manually with the "Added to savings" button.

Does it work offline?

Yes, core features work locally on your device: journal, history, goals, and charts.

What if I did not transfer the money after all?

Do not tap "Added to savings" until the transfer is real. If you confirmed by mistake, you can revert the entry in History.

How does Premium / trial work?

You get 10 free confirmed transfers. From the 11th, Premium applies; if eligible, you can start a 14-day trial.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Data is stored locally, with nothing sent externally. The app works without accounts and without ad trackers.

Can I export data?

Yes. You can export data to CSV (with a date range) and save the file locally or to iCloud Drive, for example.
